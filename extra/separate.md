
떨어지다 = something becomes separated from a higher, attached, expected, acceptable, or close state.


KEY PATTERNS

N에서 떨어지다: fall / detach from

N에 떨어지다: fail / be eliminated

수준·실력·품질이 떨어지다: be inferior

결정·판결이 떨어지다: be finalized

떨어져 있다: be distant / located away
