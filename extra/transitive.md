Something emerges or happens, often by itself

|------------------|-----------------------------|---------------------------|-------------------------------------|-------------------------------|

| 냄새가 나다      | a smell comes out           | 이상한 냄새가 나요.        | There’s a strange smell.            | Very common                   |
