걸리다: to be caught, stopped, or affected by something (often unintentionally)

| Verb / Expression | Meaning                              | Korean Example                    | English Translation                         | Side Note                               |
|------------------|--------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------|
| 걸리다           | to be caught / stuck                 | 옷이 문에 걸렸어요.               | My clothes got caught on the door.           | Physical snag                           |
| 시간이 걸리다    | to take time                         | 시간이 많이 걸려요.               | It takes a lot of time.                     | Very common pattern                     |
| ~시간 걸리다     | duration                             | 두 시간 걸렸어요.                 | It took two hours.                          | No particle needed                      |
| 감기에 걸리다    | to catch a cold                      | 감기에 걸렸어요.                 | I caught a cold.                            | Illness affects subject                |
| 병에 걸리다      | to get a disease                     | 심각한 병에 걸렸어요.             | He got a serious disease.                   | Formal/medical                          |
| 중독에 걸리다    | to become addicted                  | 게임 중독에 걸렸어요.             | I became addicted to games.                 | Passive state                           |
| 법에 걸리다      | to be caught by the law              | 불법이라 법에 걸려요.             | It’s illegal, so it’s against the law.      | System-based                            |
| 규칙에 걸리다    | to violate a rule                   | 규칙에 걸렸어요.                 | I broke the rules.                          | Often used in schools                  |
| 검사에 걸리다    | to be flagged in a test              | 보안 검사에 걸렸어요.             | I was flagged in the security check.        | Screening/filtering                    |
| 필터에 걸리다    | to be filtered                       | 스팸 필터에 걸렸어요.             | It got caught in the spam filter.           | Digital context                         |
| 눈에 걸리다      | to catch one’s eye                  | 저 간판이 눈에 걸려요.             | That sign catches my eye.                   | Visual attention                        |
| 귀에 걸리다      | to sound odd / catch attention       | 그 말이 귀에 걸렸어요.             | That comment stuck in my ear.               | Often negative nuance                  |
| 마음에 걸리다    | to bother / linger emotionally       | 그 말이 마음에 걸려요.             | That comment bothers me.                    | Emotional residue                      |
| 벌금이 걸리다    | to be fined / penalty applies        | 벌금이 걸릴 수 있어요.             | You could be fined.                         | Passive counterpart to 벌금을 걸다     |

| Mistake Type                  | Incorrect Example                 | Correct Example                   | Why It's Wrong / Fix                             |
|------------------------------|----------------------------------|-----------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Using 하다 for duration      | 두 시간 했어요.                  | 두 시간 걸렸어요.                | Duration requires 걸리다, not 하다              |
| Adding unnecessary particle  | 두 시간이 걸렸어요.              | 두 시간 걸렸어요.                | Duration nouns often drop 이/가                 |
| Using 들다 for illness       | 감기에 들었어요.                | 감기에 걸렸어요.                | 걸리다 is standard for disease                  |
| Using 되다 for law/rules     | 법에 됐어요.                    | 법에 걸렸어요.                  | Meaning is “caught by a system”                 |
| Active verb for attention    | 광고를 잡았어요.                | 광고가 눈에 걸렸어요.            | 걸리다 is passive attention                     |
| Wrong subject focus          | 내가 시간 걸렸어요.              | 이 일은 시간이 걸려요.           | Time is the subject, not the person              |
